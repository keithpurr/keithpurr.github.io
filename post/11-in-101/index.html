11 in 101

Apr 21, 2016 #wishlist

This post is inspired by the post “101 in 1001” by petitepearstyle. Since 1001 days can be quite a long time (nearly 3 years!), I decided to come up with a “11 in 101” list for the next 3 months, try to stick to it and see what I think on 31 July.

  1. Complete my roadtest.
  2. Take 1 step class and try a new one (aeal silks/ rock climbing classes seem a lot of fun!).
  3. Enjoy 3 new books for inspiration.
  4. Try the “loose effortless” hair look.
  5. Take a cool pic.
  6. Go on a picnic.
  7. Spend a weekend completely offline.
  8. Watch An Inspector Calls (if any good + And Then There Were None)
  9. Come up with a plan of visiting Europe/ Japan.
  10. Make significant progress in master thesis.
  11. Try out hyped restaurants in Saskatoon.

Less procrastination and live in the moment:).
